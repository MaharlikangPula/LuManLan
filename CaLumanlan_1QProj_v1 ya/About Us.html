Our Story

She-He-We began in 2025 when a group of passionate advocates saw ongoing gender inequalities in their communities. Their informal conversations as friends from different fields, such as education and business, soon grew into a formal organization. The founders, who came from law, social work, business, and academia, shared a vision to create real change and ensure equal opportunities for everyone, regardless of gender.

We started with small community workshops and have grown into an advocacy platform that now helps shape policy at many levels. Gender equality matters to everyone, not just women. By focusing on research, working with communities, and building partnerships, we have helped change policies in more than 15 countries and improved the lives of thousands. We bring together local activism and policy work so that people most affected by gender inequality have a say in important decisions.

Meet Our Founder

Yeshua Red O. Lumanlan
Founder & Executive Director

Yeshua Red O. Lumanlan brings over 15 years (my whole life) of experience in international development and gender advocacy to She-He-We. With a PhD in Social Policy from Oxford University and extensive fieldwork across Asia, Africa, and Latin America, she has dedicated her career to understanding and addressing the root causes of gender inequality.

Before founding She-He-We, Yeshua worked with the United Nations Development Programme, participated in international MUNs and served as a senior policy advisor to several governments on gender mainstreaming initiatives. Her research on economic empowerment of women has been published in leading academic journals, and she frequently speaks at international conferences on gender equality and sustainable development. Yeshua’s multilingual abilities and cross-cultural expertise have been instrumental in building She-He-We’s global network of partners and advocates. (None of these information are real.)
